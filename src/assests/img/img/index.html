About

Polardot 3D Survey Pro is a cloud-based 3D Drone Data Analytics Platform.

Our solution enables construction companies/organizations & drone mapping service providers to document all their sites from setting out and implementation, to project close-out, saving on time and cost while increasing accuracy, reliability, and safety.

FEATURES

Reduce Costs

- Potentially high cost change orders are avoided due to early deviation detection.

- Save on expensive hardware to run drone software.

Save Time

- Day to day progress monitoring time reduces.

- Improved communication and collaboration.

Increase Accuracy

- Higher quality and accurate progress reporting, which enables better decision making.

Enhance Safety

- Avoid manual inspections to potentially dangerous job-sites/terrain.

Unify & Explore your site Data in one platform.

  • 2D/3D CAD/BIM design or as built data.
  • Bathymetric survey data.
  • Drone topographic survey data.
  • Lidar point cloud data.
  • Drone images.
  • GPS/survey equipment data.

Rely on your own impartial data for correct estimates, site analysis and payments.

  • Accurately measure volume, areas, heights, and distances using survey grade accuracy.
  • Compare as-built to as-designed with design plan overlays.
  • 3D Cross-section analysis, plotting and export.
  • Export measurements in common GIS and CAD formats.
  • Visualize contours,2D as built data, 3D meshes, pointclouds, orthomosaic maps and digital elevation models in one platform.
  • Quantify stockpile material inventory.
  • Analyze construction deviation.

Perform routine inspection remotely, quickly and safely.

  • Conduct detailed inspection and analysis from high-resolution imagery in a fraction of the time, reducing downtime.
  • Inspect hard-to-reach areas without putting your team in danger.

Collaborate from Anywhere using Scalable Cloud Infrastructure.

  • Explore maps, photos on any device.
  • Give instant visibility to your data and status of your project.
  • Connect your team with an interactive, up-to-date visual record of your project.
  • Share your data with ease, while controlling the level of access to individual projects.
